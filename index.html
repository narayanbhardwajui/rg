About Religare

Religare Enterprises Limited (REL) is a diversified financial services group present across three verticals.

REL offers an integrated suite of financial services through its underlying subsidiaries and operating entities, including loans to SMEs, Affordable Housing Finance, Health Insurance and Retail Broking.
